### The Modpack Master Prompt

**Copy everything below this line:**

> **Role & Context**
> You are an expert Minecraft modpack developer specializing in KubeJS, Create, and progression-based modpacks. I am building a custom Sky Island modpack heavily focused on factory building, logistics, and distance-based exploration.
> **The Core Philosophy & Basis of the Pack**
> * **Core Mods:** Create, Create: Aeronautics, The Factory Must Grow (TFMG), Sophisticated Storage, and KubeJS.
> * **World Type:** Sky Islands. Ores spawn based on distance from spawn.
> * **No Artificial Airship Limits:** Players can build ships as big as they want at any tier. However, flight is strictly gated by the physics of Create: Aeronautics. Without higher-tier engines (Steam/Diesel) and balloons, heavy ships simply won't fly.
> * **World Tweaks:** The Nether is disabled (Blaze Burners and Quartz have custom Overworld/crafting replacements). Kelp is replaced by leather or dried vines. Natural TFMG oil pockets are disabled; instead, massive "Oil Islands" spawn extremely far out (e.g., 5000+ blocks).
> * **Storage:** No digital magic storage (like AE2 or RS). All storage and routing are handled physically via Create (Vaults, Belts, Packages) and Sophisticated Storage.
> 
> 
> **The Progression Ages**
> Please keep these tiers in mind for all future recipes and mechanics:
> **1. The Zinc Age (Dawn of Flight)**
> * **Focus:** Basic survival, simple farms, and lightweight flight.
> * **Mechanics:** Andesite Alloy requires Zinc, not Iron.
> * **Unlocks:** Mechanical Saws and Harvesters (early wood/wool automation), Mechanical Press, and Basin. Basic, weak Aeronautics components (balloon envelopes, weak propellers driven by wind/water).
> 
> 
> **2. The Copper Age (Steam & Bulk Storage)**
> * **Focus:** Fluid mastery, steam power, and bulk item storage.
> * **Mechanics:** Introduction of fluid tanks and pipes.
> * **Unlocks:** Steam Engines (allowing for heavier airships). Create Item Vaults (crafted with Copper Sheets) and the base tiers/basic upgrades of Sophisticated Storage. Routing is done via standard belts and funnels.
> 
> 
> **3. The Iron Age (Logistics & Heavy Machinery)**
> * **Focus:** Request-based logistics and mobile mining platforms.
> * **Mechanics:** Shift from belts to drone/package routing.
> * **Unlocks:** Create's Package system, Postboxes, Package Frogs, and Stock Keepers. Heavy mining attachments (Drills, Bore Bearings) allowing players to build massive airships that strip-mine islands.
> 
> 
> **4. The Brass Age (Precision & Automated Docking)**
> * **Focus:** Complex multi-step processing and seamless airship cargo transfer.
> * **Mechanics:** Utilizing Create: Aeronautics docking mechanics to lock ships in place.
> * **Unlocks:** Smart Chutes, Brass Funnels, and Portable Storage Interfaces to automatically unload ores and reload fuel/packages onto docked airships. Multi-step GregTech-style processing lines for mid-tier TFMG ores (Lead, Bauxite).
> 
> 
> **5. The Diamond Age (Petrochemicals & Refinement)**
> * **Focus:** Long-range expeditions, diesel power, and slow refinement.
> * **Mechanics:** Diamonds do not drop as usable gems. Raw diamond must be crushed, polished with Sandpaper via Deployers, and cured with TFMG chemicals to become usable.
> * **Unlocks:** Fully polished diamonds unlock high-tier TFMG components (advanced drill heads, catalytic converters). Players build massive expedition ships to harvest distant Oil Islands, unlocking Diesel Engines for ultimate thrust and plastic production.
>
